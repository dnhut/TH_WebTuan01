One Particular book which is recomended reading is The Street Lawyer by John Grisham .This book is about a lawyer who begins re-evaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm. Consequently, he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets , and realizes the harsh existence of the homeless, and vow to give them a chance in the courts. The Street Lawyer is a great book. It is well written and intersting . Other books by John Grisham include The Frim. The Pelican Brief. and The Client
